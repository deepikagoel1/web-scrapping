    Dr. Navneet VermaPrincipal

    Hardwar Educational College, Haridwar is affiliated with Hemwati Nandan Bahuguna Garhwal University, Garhwal. It is approved by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). College offers Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com), Master of Commerce (M.Com) and Master of International Business (MIB) courses. College has 5000 books in the library.

    The maine mission of the college is to promote and support high quality education, training and research in Management and Commerce and to redefine the Management education and to provide trained Management, which meet the needs of users and Industries. College envision itself as a model business school forming professionals. We educate students to become excellent and ethical Economists, Entrepreneurs and Business managers who are socially responsible and globally-oriented. To develop outstanding managerial talent capable of providing enlightened and effective leadership.
